RULES:

Objective:

The aim of the game is to beat the dealer without going over 21.

Card Values:

Numbered cards are worth their face value, face cards (King, Queen, Jack) are worth 10, and Aces can be either 1 or 11, depending on what's best for your hand.

Getting Started:

You and the dealer are dealt two cards each. Your cards are face up, but one of the dealer's cards is face down.

Hit or Stand:

After your initial cards are dealt, you have the choice to "hit" (take another card) or "stand" (keep your current hand). The goal is to get as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Dealer's Turn:

Once you've made your moves, it's the dealer's turn. They must hit until they reach a total of 17 or higher. If they go over 21, you win!

Winning Hands:

If your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's without going over, you win! Blackjack (an Ace and a 10-value card) pays 3 to 2, while other winning hands pay even money.

Payout:

Get that sweet feeling of victory with a payout of 3 to 2 when you hit Blackjack!

Enjoy Responsibly:

Remember, while Blackjack is exciting, it's important to gamble responsibly. Set limits and stick to them for a fun and enjoyable experience every time.
